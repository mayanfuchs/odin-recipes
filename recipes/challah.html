The One&Only Challah

A mix of everyones recipes gained over the years.

Makes 4 Small challot, or 2 medium.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 Cup Warm water
  • 1 x Sachet yeast
  • 1/2 Cup Sugar
  • 1 1/2 Tsp salt
  • 1/4 Cup oil
  • 1 x egg
  • 4 Cups flour

Steps

  1. Mix water, sugar & yeast in a bowl, Leave to rise for a few minutes
  2. Slowly add egg, oil & salt
  3. Add flour, one cup at a time depending on consistency
  4. Add more flour if needed, until dough has a sprin back
  5. Cover with wet towel and leave to rise

    6. Once risen make the blessing and take off a piece of challah - approx 30g - burn it on its own in the oven

  6. Braid challah
  7. Leave to rise a few more minutes
  8. Bake for 25 minutes, until golden brown
