Lasagna

Best Party Lasagna

Don't look any further - this is the only lasagna recipe you will ever need!

Don't be the guy that brings a tv meal lasagna to the party

Ingredients

  • 1 x box of lasagne pasta sheets
  • 1 x chopped onion
  • 1 x jar of Nona's pomodoro sauce
  • 1 x jar Nona's bachamel sauce
  • 500grams grass feed beef mince
  • Cheese - as much as you like

Steps

  1. Place pasta sheets in water for 20 minutes
  2. Saute onions for 5 minutes in a pan, add the mince and cook till browned
  3. Add Nona's pomodoro sauce and cook till hot through
  4. Place pasta sheets on bottom of a baking tray and then add a layer of cooked mince then add a layer of bachamel sauce
  5. repeat step 4 until the backing tray is full
  6. Finish with a generous amount of bachamel and a layer of cheese
  7. Cook in 180ยบ oven for 45 minutes
