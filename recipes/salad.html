Israeli salad the real way

Make your Israeli salad the proper way that last in the fridge.

No seeds! serves 4!

Ingredients

  • 1 x cucumber
  • 1 x red onion
  • 2 x large tomatoes

Steps

  1. Slice cucumber in to two length ways
  2. Remove the seeds from the cucumber middle by sliced a triangle into the cucumber, length ways
  3. Chop cucumber into small cubes
  4. Slice tomato in two and remove inside from the tomatoes
  5. Slice tomatoes into cubes
  6. Finely chop onion
  7. Mix in a bowl and add dressing of your choice
